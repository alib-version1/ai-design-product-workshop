# Step 4: Decide What to Prototype

**Tool:** M365 Copilot

Narrow down. If you were going to build a quick prototype this afternoon — what would you show?

## Prompt to copy

```
We have limited time to prototype a solution for citizens checking application progress.

Problem: Citizens cannot see where their application is. They phone, wait, and get inconsistent answers.
Persona: [paste your persona from Step 2]

Suggest:
1. A minimum viable journey (3-5 screens)
2. What to include vs leave out
3. The single most important thing the prototype must demonstrate

This is a prototype to communicate the concept — not a production service.
```

## What to look for

- Is the scope realistic — could you actually build 3–5 simple pages this afternoon?
- Does it focus on the core user need: seeing where my application is?
- What would your Product Manager instinct tell you to cut?
