# Sample Persona: David Okonkwo

This is an example persona created using M365 Copilot and refined to feel specific and grounded. A good persona should be recognisable to a User Researcher — someone you could imagine meeting in research.

---

**Name:** David Okonkwo

**Age:** 58

**Situation:** Recently made redundant after 22 years. Applied for financial support for the first time in his life. Lives alone. Has a basic smartphone but prefers paper and phone.

## Goals

- Know whether his application is being processed or if something has gone wrong
- Not have to chase — he finds it stressful and worries he is being a nuisance
- Get a clear answer about when he will hear back so he can plan

## Frustrations

- Has called three times in two weeks. Different information each time.
- First call: "it's in the queue." Second call: "it's been assigned." Third call: "I can't see any update on the system."
- Waited 40 minutes on hold before getting through the second time
- Never received written confirmation after submitting — not sure it went through properly
- Does not understand what "assigned for review" means or how long that takes

## Context

Checks his phone for emails several times a day hoping for news. Would use an online tracker if it existed but it would need to be simple — no account creation, no complicated verification. His daughter helps with technology sometimes but he does not want to bother her with this.

## Digital confidence

Low-medium. Can use email and basic websites. Gets stuck when things need account creation, passwords, or multiple steps.

## Quote

> "I've worked and paid my taxes for 30 years and I can't even find out if anyone's looking at my application. It makes you feel invisible."
