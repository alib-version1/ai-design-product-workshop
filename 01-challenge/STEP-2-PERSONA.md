# Step 2: Create a Persona

**Tool:** M365 Copilot

Generate a persona — then challenge whether it feels real.

## Prompt to copy

```
Service: Checking the progress of a government application
Main users: Citizens who have submitted an application and are waiting — this could be a licence, a benefit claim, a permit, a court-related application, or a registration
Key problem: No way to check progress online. Only option is to phone, which is slow and gives inconsistent answers.

Create a realistic persona:
- Name, age, situation
- Goals
- Frustrations with the current experience
- Context (when and how they try to check progress)
- A quote capturing how they feel
- Digital confidence level

A real person, not a segment. Someone whose circumstances make the wait particularly difficult.
```

## What to look for

- Is this a specific person with a specific situation — or a generic category?
- Are the frustrations grounded in actual interactions (calls, letters, waiting)?
- Would a User Researcher recognise this person?

## Now refine it

```
Make this person less digitally confident. Someone who would usually ask a family member for help with online services but is dealing with this on their own.
```
