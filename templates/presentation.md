# Team Presentation Template

Each team has 5–7 minutes. Use this structure to keep your presentation focused.

---

## 1. The problem (1 minute)

What service, what user need, why it matters.

- What is the service?
- What is going wrong for users?
- Why does solving this matter — for users and for the organisation?

---

## 2. The user (1 minute)

Your persona. Their situation, frustration, and what good looks like for them.

- Who is this person?
- What are they trying to do?
- What is their experience today?

---

## 3. The approach (1 minute)

Where you intervened in the service. Your hypothesis.

- Where in the service did you focus?
- What is your hypothesis — if we build this, what changes for the user?
- What did you deliberately leave out?

---

## 4. The prototype (2–3 minutes)

Live demo. Walk us through the journey.

- Show the prototype in order — click through it, do not just describe it
- Explain what the user sees, does, and decides at each step
- Point out the content and design decisions you made

---

## 5. What you learned (1 minute)

Where AI helped. Where your expertise was essential. What you would do next.

- Where did AI tools speed things up or surprise you?
- Where did your professional judgement override the AI?
- If you had another day, what would you do next?
